# Privacy Policy for Map Chat

**Last updated: March 5, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Map Chat** ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide
- **Account information**: When you register, we collect your email address, password (encrypted), and username.
- **Profile information**: Profile photo and biography that you optionally upload.

### b. Information Collected Automatically
- **Location data**: With your explicit permission, we collect your real-time GPS location to show you nearby users on the map and let others near you discover you. Location is only collected while the app is in use.
- **Messages**: Chat messages you send to other users are stored to enable communication.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Display your position on the discovery map to nearby users
- Facilitate real-time chat between users
- Authenticate your identity securely
- Improve app performance and user experience

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using **Google Firebase**, which includes:
- **Firebase Authentication** – for secure login
- **Cloud Firestore** – for user profiles and messages
- **Firebase Storage** – for profile photos and shared media

Firebase complies with GDPR and SOC 2 standards. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

---

## 4. Location Data

We access your device's GPS location **only when the app is open and you grant permission**. Location data is used solely to:
- Show your position on the Nearby Map
- Help other users near you discover you

You can revoke location access at any time from your device settings. If you revoke access, the discovery map feature will not be available.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties except:
- **Google Firebase** (our backend infrastructure provider)
- When required by law or legal process

---

## 6. Data Retention

- Your account data is retained as long as your account exists.
- You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen, which will permanently remove your data.
- Chat messages are deleted automatically based on the disappearing message timer you set.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Map Chat is not intended for users under the age of **13**. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us immediately.

---

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- Withdraw consent for location access at any time

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
